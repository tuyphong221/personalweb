ABOUT AN NGUYEN

Meet An Nguyen, Saigon's quiet achiever in design. Seven years of journeying through the nooks and crannies of non-profits and digital corporations have equipped him with a keen eye for potential—a gift he employs to nurture brands and products.

An steps lightly in the realm of Web3, sharing his talent with purpose-driven startups from various domains: digital banking, DeFi, cryptocurrencies, AI, and green energy. His hands mold 3D visualizations and craft dApps, subtly shaping the contour of our digital tomorrow.

Inspiration whispers to An through Krav Maga, Vajrayana Buddhism, and his travels. From modeling to prototyping, every piece of his work is a humble ode to life's diverse tapestry, echoing with the mellow notes of a decentralized, Web3 future. With An, the extraordinary is found in the ordinary, quietly inspiring the dawn of a new digital era.
